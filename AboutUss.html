A Multi-Gym Database Management System




Goal:Our goal is to implement a well-structured database management system, and web-based application that will aid in the daily operations of a gym.








Users:There are 4 types of users:

  • Owners: Gym Owners can upload info of their gyms to our database.
  • Clients: Clients can choose their preferred gym and register. They can view their improvement reports as well.
  • Trainers: They can register to their respective gyms to upload their info to our database
  • Nutritionists: They can register to their respective gyms to upload their info to our database




Features:

  • Trainer:
    • Can register and upload their info
    • Can view their clients
    • Can view their clients improvement report and update it.

  • Client:
    • Can register and upload their information.
    • Can view gyms based on location.
    • Can select and register to their preferred gym.
    • Can view their trainer and nutritionist contact information.
  • Owner:
    • Can register and upload their gym's info to the database.
    • Can remove trainers and nutritionists info from their gym.
    • Can insert and update info of packages and equipment of their gym.
  • Nutritionist:
    • Can register and upload their information.
    • Can view their clients.
    • Can make diet plans for their clients.
